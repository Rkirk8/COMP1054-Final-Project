About Us

Welcome to Peak Ski & Boards, your ultimate destination for all things skiing and snowboarding! Nestled in the heart of Snowtown, we are passionate about winter sports and dedicated to providing our customers with top-quality gear, apparel, and accessories. Whether you are a seasoned pro or just starting out, our knowledgeable staff is here to help you find the perfect equipment to enhance your mountain adventures. At Peak Ski & Boards, we believe in offering exceptional service, expert advice, and a wide range of products to suit every budget and skill level. Join us and experience the thrill of the slopes with confidence and style!

Our Story

Peak Ski & Boards was founded by a group of avid skiers and snowboarders who wanted to share their love for the mountains with the community. Our journey began in 2003, when we opened our doors with a mission to create a one-stop shop for winter sports enthusiasts. Over the years, we have grown and evolved, but our core values remain the same: quality, expertise, and a passion for the slopes.

Our Products

We offer a comprehensive selection of skis, snowboards, boots, bindings, helmets, goggles, and apparel from the most trusted brands in the industry. Whether you're looking for the latest technology or timeless classics, you'll find everything you need at Peak Ski & Boards. Our inventory is carefully curated to ensure that we provide the best products for every type of skier and snowboarder.

Expert Advice

Our team of experts is always ready to assist you with personalized recommendations and professional advice. We understand that choosing the right gear can make a significant difference in your performance and enjoyment on the slopes. That's why we take the time to understand your needs and preferences, ensuring that you leave our store with the perfect equipment.

Services

At Peak Ski & Boards, we offer a range of services to keep your gear in top condition. From ski and snowboard tuning to boot fitting and custom insoles, our experienced technicians use the latest tools and techniques to provide high-quality maintenance and repairs. We also offer rental services for those who want to try out the latest gear before making a purchase.

Community Engagement

We believe in giving back to the community that supports us. Peak Ski & Boards is proud to sponsor local events, support ski and snowboard clubs, and participate in environmental initiatives to preserve the beauty of our mountains. Join us for our annual demo days, clinics, and workshops to connect with fellow enthusiasts and improve your skills.
